Bottega Nova — handmade leather goods

Our craft

Bottega Nova has hand-cut and stitched leather goods in Florence since 1978. Every piece in this collection is made to order by a team of six artisans, using vegetable-tanned leather sourced from a single tannery we have worked with for three generations.

This season pairs our classic silhouettes with a new chestnut-brown tanning batch, available while stock lasts.
