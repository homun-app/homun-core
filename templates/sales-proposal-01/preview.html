Logistics Optimization Proposal

Prepared for Aurora Logistics

Context & needs

Aurora Logistics operates 24 distribution sites across Italy and the DACH region. Plant managers currently rely on end-of-week spreadsheets to catch downtime, so costly stoppages often stay invisible until the monthly close.

The operations team asked for a way to see cost-of-downtime in real time and route alerts to the right shift lead, without adding new hardware to the line.

Proposed solution

Kite Analytics deploys plug-and-play adapters against Aurora's existing PLCs and MES, live within two weeks per site.

  • Live cost-of-downtime dashboard for every plant manager
  • Alerts routed automatically to the on-duty shift lead
  • Monthly variance report delivered straight to the COO's inbox
